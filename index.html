    • About Green Thumb
    • Our Vision and Mission
    • Vision

      • To enable all plant owners to meet their full "growth" potential. Allowing all homes to be covered in foliage that is properly cared for and vibrant!

    • Mission

      • Offer a user friendly and helpful interface to provide end users an informative platform to find house plant information. As well as locate stores within close proximity that sell house plants.

    • What we deliver!

      • A fully functioning visually appealing application that meets are users needs. Enabling them to find care and use information about house plants and provide a local solution to possibly purchase those plants.
